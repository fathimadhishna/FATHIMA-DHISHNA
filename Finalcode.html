Projects

Community participation in wetland conservation:A case study of Kadalundi-Vallikkunni community reserve

This case study analyses the participation of community in conservation of a wetland, Kadalundi-Vallikkunnu community reserve in Kerala.It particularly evaluates the ecological significance of the reserve, ecosystem services of it and its economic value by assessing its benefit to the local community.

Comparison of Electrochemical oxidation and ozonation for the degradation of Propylparaben

This thesis is a comparitive study of two advanced oxidation process technologies, Electrochemical oxidation and Ozonation for the degradation of an emerging contaminant Propylparaben in water.This study evaluates the feasibility of using Chemical oxygen demand (COD) and UV visible spectroscopy as environmental monitoring techniques for the study.

Source to sink: A possible mechanism of transport of Mangiferin in Salacia brunoniana

and potentiality of leaf extract in anti-cancerous studies

This project investigates the phytochemistry and pharmacological potential of Salacia brunoniana focusing on the source to sink transport of mangiferin and evaluating its presence in leaves as an alternative to root extraction.
